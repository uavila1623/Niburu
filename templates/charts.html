To create a regional chart follow the steps below:

  1. Choose the state that your city and county is based in.
  2. Then choose the weather type that you want to base the chart on.
  3. Next choose the chart type you want to display.
  4. After that enter the city that you want to base the data on.
  5. Finally enter the county that the city is based in.
  6. Press the "Make Chart" button and wait for the chart to display.

Some brief information on the data being displayed in the chart. The chart is showing how many times the weather type occurred in the region from 2016-21.
